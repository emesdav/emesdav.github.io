About Me

My name is Emmanuel and this is my attempt at sharing my study journey in an e-portfolio

The easiest way to introduce myself is in the words of Confucius "I hear and I forget. I see and I remember. I do and I understand." (Confucius, Chinese - Philosopher 551 BC - 479 BC). I am very practical and less theoretical although I find the balance between the 2. I have had love for computers and airplanes for as long as I can remember. And with over 10years of experience working in the field of software development and related I intend to go as far as possible and effect the world around me starting from an investment of good study in myself.
